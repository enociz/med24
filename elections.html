2020 MUMUS Rep Elections

Hey! Here are some basic instructions and information regarding the elections for the MUMUS first year reps of 2020.

  • Venue:

    The link/instructions will be posted here on how to vote and where when they're up on the Facebook group

  • Time:

    Voting opens at 6PM (AEST) on 27/04/2020

    Voting closes at 6PM (AEST) on 04/05/2020

    Results announced after 11:59PM (AEST) on 04/05/2020

  • Each candidate will have a short statement on the voting form/ballot which will help you to understand about them and their policies
  • The voting system is the instant run-off system (and so you rank the candidates by your preference from 1 to X)

Year 1 Academic Representative

Responsibilities:

  • Sit on the Academic Committee.
  • Advocate for the best interests of their year level to various staff of the University with regards to academic and assessment matters.
  • Assist in planning, promotion and implementation of MUMUS events including Revision Lecture programs.
  • Provide input into planning of academic events and MUMUS academic policy where required.
  • Sit on relevant faculty committees and report back to the Academic Vice President.

This representative needs to be someone who you believe will represent you and your needs well on the faculty committees with a level of maturity and knowledgeability, and who you trust to provide you with quality opportunities to improve your academic side this year.

Year 1 Social Representatives

Responsibilities:

  • Sit on the Social Committee.
  • Represent the interests of their year level and express those opinions at appropriate forums.
  • Assist in the planning, promotion and implementation of MUMUS events as appropriate, including both alcoholic and non-alcoholic events.

This representative needs to be someone who you believe will represent you and your needs well on the faculty committees with a level of maturity and knowledgeability, and who you trust to provide you with quality opportunities to improve your social side this year.
